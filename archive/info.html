About Everyday Supply Co.

Everyday Supply Co. is a growing fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) supplier focused on delivering essential, affordable products to the people and businesses that rely on them every day. The company was built from the ground up with a simple idea: daily-use products should be accessible, reliable, and reasonably priced for everyone.

We directly supply households, small businesses, student communities, and individual customers with practical everyday items. By focusing on products that people use regularly, we aim to simplify access to essential goods while building long-term relationships with our customers.

Today, Everyday Supply Co. operates in both Johannesburg and Durban, serving customers across urban neighborhoods while also working toward reaching rural and underserved communities.

To make our products even more accessible, we offer free delivery across Johannesburg and Durban, ensuring that customers receive what they need quickly and conveniently.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted everyday supplier that improves access to affordable consumer goods across South Africa. We aim to build a distribution network that reaches both urban centers and rural communities, ensuring that essential products are available to everyone regardless of location or income level.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and impactful products that support the daily lives of our customers. We focus on serving households, individuals, and businesses with products that are practical, accessible, and consistently available. By keeping operations simple and customer-focused, we strive to deliver value while building long-term trust.

Terms of Service

Welcome to Everyday Supply Co. By using our website and services, you agree to the following basic terms:

  • Orders & Fulfillment: All orders placed via our website or WhatsApp are subject to product availability. We reserve the right to modify or cancel orders if stock is unavailable.
  • Pricing: All prices are displayed in South African Rands (ZAR). We strive to keep our prices accurate and updated, but reserve the right to correct pricing errors.
  • Delivery: We offer 100% free delivery across our serviced areas in Johannesburg and Durban. Delivery timelines will be communicated to you upon order confirmation via WhatsApp.
  • Returns & Refunds: If you receive damaged or incorrect goods, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or credit for valid claims. Due to the nature of FMCG food products, returns on opened items are generally not accepted unless defective.

Privacy Policy

At Everyday Supply Co., we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

  • Information We Collect: When you place an order, we collect necessary information such as your name, delivery address, phone number, and order details.
  • How We Use Your Info: Your data is used exclusively for order fulfillment, arranging delivery, and communicating with you regarding your purchases (usually via WhatsApp).
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your delivery details are only shared with our internal delivery drivers to ensure your goods arrive securely.
  • Security: We implement appropriate security measures to ensure your data is stored safely. You have the right to request the deletion or updating of your records at any time by contacting us.
