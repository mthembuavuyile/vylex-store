06 — Usage Rules

Logo Guidelines

To maintain brand integrity, the logo must always have clear space and must never be distorted, recoloured outside approved variations, or placed on clashing backgrounds.


✓ Do — Clear Space
↕ x ↔ x
vylex.
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "v" on all sides.
✕ Don't — Recolour
vylex.
Never use unapproved colours or alter the logo's component colours.
✕ Don't — Distort
vylex.
Never stretch, compress, rotate, or otherwise distort the logo.
✕ Don't — Low Contrast BG
vylex.
Never place the logo on a background with insufficient contrast.
✕ Don't — Add Effects
vylex.
Never apply drop shadows, glows, blurs, or visual effects to the logo.
✕ Don't — Wrong Typeface
VYLEX
Never recreate the logo using a different typeface. Use official files only.
07 — Motion & Animation

Motion Principles

Motion should feel considered and purposeful. We use easing curves that reflect confidence — never bouncy or playful. Transitions should orient, not distract.


Default Transition
200ms ease
Hover states, focus rings, colour changes.
Enter / Appear
300ms ease-out
Modals, dropdowns, tooltips appearing.
Exit / Dismiss
200ms ease-in
Always faster than entrance.
Page Transition
350ms ease-in-out
Between pages or major view changes.
Lift (Cards)
translateY(-5px)
Primary hover interaction for card components.
Reduced Motion
prefers-reduced-motion
All animations must respect this media query.
